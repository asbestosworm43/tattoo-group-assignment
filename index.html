Welcome To FeSaTo Tattoo!

Info

We are a group of tattoo artists working with several styles and can travel to studios all over Sweden.

Style

Tattooing is a diverse field of art, and there are many different styles to choose from, depending on your taste. A tattoo style is the general look of the tattoo, which means that one object can have many different looks. We work with Traditional & Old School, Neo Traditional, Watercolor, Blackwork, Aesthetic, Sketch and Realism.

Sharpness

Our needles are extra sharp to provide you with the finest lines and cleanest edges.

Crazyness

No tattoo idea is too crazy - contact us with your thoughts and we will make your dream come true!
